Easy Vegan Hot Chocolate

Delicious in the winter!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup water
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 3 cups soy milk

Steps

  1. Get a small saucepan
  2. Bring water, sugar and cocoa to a boil
  3. Pour in soy milk and stir until combined and heated through, about 3 minutes.
